How it started

A workshop, not a warehouse

Volt & Grain started in a garage where the founders kept rebuilding a secondhand turntable instead of replacing it. That habit — naming every part, keeping it repairable — became the whole company. We still publish the spec sheet for anything we sell, down to the wood species and the driver diameter.

We're a small team: five people who test, box, and answer support chats. If something breaks, you'll talk to someone who knows which screw to loosen.
